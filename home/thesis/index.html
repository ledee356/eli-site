SHALL PASS
A SHORT FILM BY
ELI LEDERBERG
This Too Shall Pass follows Noel as she struggles with her mental illness. In an attempt to address some of
the issues surrounding it, she reaches out to her
estranged father.

But when their meeting quickly turns south, Noel
ends up experiencing a mental episode, leading her
to question whether or not her father deserves such
a present role in her life.
