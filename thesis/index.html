This Too Shall Pass follows Noel as she struggles with her mental illness. In an attempt to address some of the issues surrounding it, she reaches out to her estranged father.

But when their meeting quickly turns south, Noel ends up experiencing a mental episode, leading her to question whether or not her father deserves such a present role in her life.

Created as a companion guide to the short film, This Too Shall Pass: A Mental Wellness Companion Guide is designed to provide strategies to help both those coping with mental illness and those looking to help support individuals dealing with mental illness.

For those coping, the guide can be read front to back. For those looking to offer support, the guide can be read back to front.
